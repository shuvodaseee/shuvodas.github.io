// 01 — About Me
The Engineer

I'm Shuvo Das, a Fire Protection & Detection Project Engineer based in Bangladesh with a background in Electrical & Electronic Engineering. I work at Lucky Automation Engineering, where I manage end-to-end fire system design and installation for commercial and industrial facilities.

My work spans the full spectrum of active fire protection: from intricate sprinkler hydraulic calculations to commissioning fire alarm control panels, designing clean agent suppression (FM-200 / CO₂) systems, and producing compliant AutoCAD drawings and BOQs.

Every project I undertake is guided by rigorous adherence to NFPA 13, 14, 20, 72, BNBC guidelines, and FSCD regulations — because life safety is not a checkbox.

🎯 Career Goal: Seeking international project opportunities across Europe and the Middle East, where I can contribute to large-scale fire protection and safety infrastructure.
